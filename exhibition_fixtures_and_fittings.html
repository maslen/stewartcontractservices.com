Exhibition Fixtures and Fittings

We can supply a host of fixtures and fittings that might be required for your exhibition needs. Plinths, shelves, banners, furniture, flooring, staging, etc. We can also offer a full electrical service for your exhibition needs, ranging from lighting, to power points, to consumer units, and organise the hire of electrical equipment such as plasma screens etc.

Plinths

We have a stock of plinths available for hire, of various sizes and shapes. Please enquire further if you need specific sizes/prices. We also offer a custom build service, for Artists and exhibitors who require specific display plinths to buy. Please ask for a quote if you require this service. We specialise in producing stacking sets of plinths to reduce transportation/storage space.

Custom Builds

We can offer a custom build service for your exhibiting needs. We have in the past designed and installed stands for trade shows all over England and Europe. But we could create you a display unit for a specific section of a show, or a set of stacking display plinths, or counters for a reception area, or even a catwalk for a fashion show. Basically we are confident that we could construct any element required for your event, just give us a call.

Popups

We can organise pop up display banners and graphics for your exhibition stands, please enquire if you require further information.
